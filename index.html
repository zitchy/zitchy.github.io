Visit Ghatkopar

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant town close to the center of Mumbai.

Top three activities to do in Ghatkopar

Eat your fill at Khau Galli

Plenty of variety in food can be found to pick your meal from at Khau Galli.

Catch a show at the ZP theatre hall

The ZP hall has great gujarati threatre showings each month.

Take a hike

The Khandoba hills are a good break from the city life where you can catch a great sunrise.
